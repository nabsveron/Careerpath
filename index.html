Who is CareerPath?

Careerpath is a registered Ugandan non-profit committed to stimulating career development for the Ugandan society. By initiating and sustaining a career system in Uganda through Career Awareness, Career Education, Work Experience Education, and Employability Education, Careerpath envisions a society where people have passionate and fulfilling work.

We aim to integrate career teaching and learning in Uganda’s education system and pride ourselves on partnerships with public and private institutions, including the Ministry of Education and Sports and National Curriculum Development Center.

Our Programs

We deliver practical career education, employer engagement, mentorship and employability training to prepare young people for meaningful work.

Career Education Program (CEP)

Support trained career teachers and counselors; develop career handbooks and run self-discovery classes that align education to career goals.

Learner–Employer Engagements Program (LEEP)

Facilitate job-shadowing and workplace visits linking learners to local employers to learn about specific career fields and work culture.

Career Mentorship Program

Recruit volunteer mentors who provide one-on-one guidance, career talks and resources to students and emerging professionals.

Employability Education

Deliver practical lessons on CV writing, interviews, careerpreneurship and 21st century skills to increase workplace readiness.

Internships & Industrial Training

Host interns, place students in workplace projects and support institutions to deliver high-quality industrial placements.

Career Talk (Let’s Talk Work)

Provide job market updates, career fairs and a career helpline that connects job seekers with employers and resources.
